Our Story

NoneCloud was founded by passionate gamers who understood the frustration of poor server performance, unreliable hosting, and lackluster support. We experienced firsthand the impact that server quality has on gaming communities, and we knew there had to be a better way.

Starting with a simple mission to provide reliable, high-performance game hosting, we've grown into a trusted provider serving thousands of gaming communities worldwide. Our journey began with Minecraft hosting, but our passion for excellence quickly expanded to include CS2, Rust, ARK, and many other popular games.

What sets us apart is our commitment to the gaming community. We're not just a hosting company – we're gamers ourselves, and we understand what it takes to create memorable gaming experiences.
