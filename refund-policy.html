Refund Policy

Clear and fair refund terms for NoneCloud hosting services

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

1. Overview

At NoneCloud, we strive to provide exceptional game hosting services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued for our hosting services, including Minecraft, CS2, and other game server hosting solutions.

Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new customers who are not satisfied with our services.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

2.1 New Customer Refunds

New customers are eligible for a full refund within the first 7 days of service activation if:

  • This is your first purchase with NoneCloud
  • The refund request is made within 7 days of service activation
  • The service has not been used excessively (fair usage applies)
  • No violations of our Terms of Service have occurred

2.2 Service Failure Refunds

Refunds may be issued for service failures including:

  • Extended downtime exceeding our SLA guarantees
  • Critical service issues that prevent normal usage
  • Hardware failures resulting in data loss (where backups fail)
  • Inability to provide the purchased service specifications

2.3 Billing Error Refunds

Full refunds will be issued for:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Incorrect billing amounts
  • Charges for services not ordered
  • Technical billing errors on our part

3. Non-Refundable Services

Important: The following services and situations are not eligible for refunds:

  • Services used for more than 7 days (except for service failures)
  • Repeat customers (those who have previously received refunds)
  • Setup fees and one-time charges
  • Domain registrations and SSL certificates
  • Third-party licenses and software
  • Services terminated due to Terms of Service violations
  • Accounts suspended for abuse or policy violations
  • Services canceled by customer request after 7 days

4. Refund Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Contact our support team via ticket system or Discord
  2. Provide your account information and order details
  3. Specify the reason for the refund request
  4. Include any relevant documentation or evidence

4.2 Refund Processing Time

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 1-3 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: 24-48 hours (subject to network confirmation)
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days

4.3 Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase. In cases where this is not possible, alternative arrangements will be made.

5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Pro-rated refunds for unused service time due to service failures
  • Downgrade refunds when switching to a lower-tier plan
  • Service credit compensation for SLA breaches
  • Refunds minus usage fees for excessive resource consumption

6. Service Level Agreement (SLA) Credits

Instead of monetary refunds, we may offer service credits for SLA breaches:

  • 99.0% - 99.8% uptime: 5% service credit
  • 95.0% - 98.9% uptime: 15% service credit
  • Below 95.0% uptime: 30% service credit

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

Important Notice: Filing a chargeback or payment dispute without first contacting our support team may result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of all services.

We encourage customers to contact us directly before initiating any payment disputes, as most issues can be resolved quickly through our support channels.

8. Refund Abuse Prevention

To prevent abuse of our refund policy:

  • Customers are limited to one refund per 12-month period
  • Accounts with multiple refund requests may be flagged for review
  • Suspected fraudulent refund requests will be investigated
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to customers who abuse our policies

9. Special Circumstances

9.1 Emergency Refunds

In exceptional circumstances, we may issue emergency refunds outside of normal policy guidelines. These are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

9.2 Goodwill Refunds

We may occasionally issue goodwill refunds for customer satisfaction purposes, even when not required by this policy.

10. Data and Service Termination

Upon refund approval:

  • Services will be terminated immediately
  • Data will be available for download for 7 days
  • Backups will be permanently deleted after the grace period
  • Account access will be revoked

11. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us:

  • Support Ticket: Available through your account dashboard
  • Email: billing@nonecloud.com
  • Discord: Join our support server
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for refund requests

12. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Customers will be notified of significant changes via email.

Fair and Transparent: Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both customers and NoneCloud. We aim to resolve all refund requests quickly and fairly while maintaining the sustainability of our services.

Customer Satisfaction: If you're experiencing issues with our services, please contact our support team first. Many problems can be resolved without needing a refund, and we're committed to ensuring your satisfaction with NoneCloud.
