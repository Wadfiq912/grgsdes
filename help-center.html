Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with NoneCloud?

Getting started is easy! Simply choose a hosting plan that fits your needs, create an account, and follow our setup wizard. You'll have access to the Pterodactyl Panel where you can install games, manage files, and configure your server. Our getting started guide walks you through every step.

What games are supported on NoneCloud?

We support 50+ popular games including Minecraft (Java & Bedrock), Counter-Strike 2, Rust, ARK: Survival Evolved, Garry's Mod, Terraria, and many more. Our platform is constantly updated to support new games as they become popular in the gaming community.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my hosting plan?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time to get more resources. Downgrades are also possible, though please note that downgrading may require reducing your server's resource usage first. All plan changes are prorated, so you only pay the difference.

How do backups work?

We provide automated daily backups for all servers, with the ability to create manual backups anytime through the Pterodactyl Panel. Backups are stored securely and can be restored with just a few clicks. Higher-tier plans include more frequent backups and longer retention periods.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 support through multiple channels: Discord community, ticket system, live chat during business hours, and email support. Our team consists of experienced gamers and server administrators who understand your needs and can help with technical issues, setup questions, and optimization advice.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new customers who are not satisfied with our services. Please review our refund policy for complete details on eligibility and the refund process.
