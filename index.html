Yes. All plans include advanced DDoS mitigation and firewall configurations to keep your servers safe.

Absolutely. Our support team can help you migrate from other providers or self-hosted setups with minimal downtime.

Automated backups are included on all plans. Configure schedules and retention right from the panel.

We’re available 24/7 via Discord and tickets. Priority support is included on Pro and Enterprise plans.
